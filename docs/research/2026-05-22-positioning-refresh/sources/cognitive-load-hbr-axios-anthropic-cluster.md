## Key Findings

- ~14% of US workers report AI-induced "mental fog" (HBR Mar 2026 — worker survey data).
- AI agents "operate like slot machines scrambling power-user brains" — variable-reinforcement
  reward pattern is the named mechanism (Axios Apr 2026).
- Heavy AI coding assistance produces a 17% reduction in skill mastery (Anthropic internal
  study, 2026) — quantified loss of expertise, not just subjective fatigue.
- Cognitive-load phenomenon is now mainstream business-press vocabulary, not a niche concern.
- The vendor itself (Anthropic) publicly acknowledges the issue, which is structurally unusual
  and increases the credibility of the broader thesis.
