## Key Findings

- ~86% of OSS maintainers are unpaid for their maintenance work, per multiple converging
  surveys (Tidelift, Indie Hackers, GitHub Octoverse).
- jdx (Jeff Dickey) went full-time on Mise in April 2026, funded through a combination of
  sponsorship, retainers, and adjacent consulting — not direct tool monetization.
- The "OSS dev tool as full-time job" pattern requires the consultancy / talks / retainer
  layer; direct revenue is consistently inadequate.
- Single-founder OSS dev tools that successfully transition to full-time look more like
  Mitchell Hashimoto's pattern (post-HashiCorp full-time OSS work, funded through
  reputation capital) than direct product monetization.
- Implication for borg: monetization, if pursued, routes through Stillpoint Labs
  consultancy work — not through borg the product.
