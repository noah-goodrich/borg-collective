## Key Findings

1. **AI SDR tools split into three categories:** fully autonomous agents (11x, Artisan,
   AiSDR), copilots (Outreach Kaia, Salesloft, Regie, Apollo AI), and intelligence layers
   (Clay, Common Room).
2. **The 11x scandal is the cautionary case for autonomous SDRs.** TechCrunch reported
   $74M raised, customer churn 70-80%, fabricated customer claims (ZoomInfo, Airtable),
   internally massaged growth numbers, $14M claimed ARR vs ~$3M actual past trial.
3. **Industry churn for AI SDR tools: 50-70% within a year.** Only 2% of companies
   successfully implement them in a sticky way.
4. **Autonomous SDRs work for narrow conditions.** High-volume top-of-funnel outbound to
   broad ICP with deal sizes < $25K, inbound lead response, re-engagement of cold leads.
5. **They consistently fail for complex enterprise sales, existing relationships,
   regulated industries, and multi-threaded deal management.**
6. **Lemkin's experiment (referenced):** replaced GTM team with 20 AI agents managed by 1.2
   humans, sent 70K emails vs 7K from human team. Result described as "better than a
   mid-pack AE or SDR, but not better than top performers" — "AI raises the floor but does
   not raise the ceiling."
