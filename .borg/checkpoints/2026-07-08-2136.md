**Two silent-blindness bugs found, fixed, and pinned with regression tests:**
1. `/usage` prints nothing and exits `0` when `USER` is unset. `cortex-wake.plist` sets **no** `EnvironmentVariables`
   — cloning it verbatim ships a permanently blind poller.
2. A native Claude pane reports `pane_current_command` as its **version** (`2.1.205`), never `claude`. The first idle
   gate matched `/^claude$/` and counted 0 while 6 panes ran. Caught only by an end-to-end run, not by green units.

**Measurement that changed a decision:** live burn rate is **~0.8%/min**, ~3x the ~1%/4min extrapolated in the spike.
An 85% threshold buys ~40 min, not ~1 hour. Do not tune off a single sample.
